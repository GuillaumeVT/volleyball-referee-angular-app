Unsubscription and account deletion

There are two ways for canceling a Volleyball Referee subscription:

  1. From the Google Play Store, follow the steps decribed on the Google support pages: Cancel, pause, or change a subscription on Google Play.
    The user account will be suspended, and the data will remain avaiable for 6 months in case of a re-subscription. Beyond this 6-month delay, both the data and account will be definitely deleted.

  2. From the Volleyball Referee website, go to your account page and fill the form for deleting your account.
    The subscription will be immediately canceled, and all the data will be immediately deleted.
